## About the Sponsor: Polymarket

Polymarket is the world's largest decentralized prediction market platform. We allow users to trade on the outcomes of real-world events—across politics, economics, pop culture, and more—using cryptocurrency. By requiring participants to back their forecasts with capital, our peer-to-peer markets aggregate news, polls, and expert opinions to surface accurate, unbiased, and real-time probabilities for the events that matter most.

## **Brief Description/Objective of Bounty**

Build a fully autonomous trading agent that monitors real-time news sources, analyzes Polymarket orderbooks, estimates predictive edge, and executes trades autonomously.

## Prize Information

$1,000

## Background Information

In prediction markets, reacting first to breaking news while accurately assessing the market's current pricing is the key to profitability. This bounty aims to crowdsource innovative approaches to AI-driven data synthesis and quantitative confidence modeling.

## Deliverables

* A public GitHub repository containing the complete, well-documented agent code.
* A detailed README specifically explaining the chosen data inputs and the mathematical/logical framework behind the confidence scoring.
* A simulated run or backtest log demonstrating the agent reacting to a specific news event, scoring its confidence, calculating its edge, and making a trade decision.

## Jugement Criteria

* **Data Ingestion Strategy:** The quality, diversity, and latency optimization of the data fed into the model.
* **Confidence Scoring Logic:** The mathematical soundness, robustness, and innovative approach of the edge-calculation algorithms.
* **Execution & Code Quality:** The reliability of orderbook analysis, trade execution logic, and overall code structure.
