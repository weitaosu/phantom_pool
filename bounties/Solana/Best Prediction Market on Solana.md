## Background Information

Prediction markets are one of the most promising applications of blockchain technology - they aggregate dispersed information into price signals that often outperform expert forecasts. Solana’s high throughput and low fees make it uniquely suited for this category, where rapid trading, instant settlement, and minimal friction are critical for accurate price discovery.

The Solana ecosystem has seen several prediction market experiments. Hedgehog Markets pioneered pooled liquidity models inspired by Uniswap’s AMM design. MetaDAO has explored futarchy-based governance, where market prices directly drive organizational decisions. And notably, Kalshi — the only CFTC-regulated prediction market exchange — has tokenized its entire market catalog on Solana through DFlow’s Prediction Markets API. Kalshi’s positions are now real SPL tokens that can be traded, borrowed, lent, or used as collateral across Solana DeFi. Builders can tap into this regulated liquidity programmatically via the DFlow API, and Kalshi is backing the ecosystem with a $2M grants program. Despite all of this infrastructure, the design space for novel prediction market mechanisms on Solana remains wide open.

## Deliverables

* Working deployment or proof of concept on Solana devnet or mainnet with at least one resolvable market.
* Public GitHub repository with complete source code.
* Detailed README covering setup instructions, market mechanism design, resolution strategy, and architecture overview.
* Demo video (~3 minutes) showing market creation, trading, and resolution.

## Jugement Criteria

* Market Design: Is the market mechanism well-designed? Does it incentivize honest information revelation and efficient price discovery?
* Innovation: How original is the approach? Does it explore new territory beyond standard binary outcome markets?
* Technical Merit: Quality of smart contract implementation, code architecture, and documentation.
* Functionality: Does the market work end-to-end? Can users create, trade, and resolve markets?
* User Experience: Is the platform intuitive enough to attract participants beyond crypto-native users?
